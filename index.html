What is V-INSPIRION ?

V-Inspiron is a 24 hrs long hackathon. V-Inspiron serves as a platform to encourage enthusiastic minds to brainstorm on solutions for challenging issues from all over India. The unified motive of this hackathon is gathering all like-minded hackers and provide a platform for innovations, where student developers can develop their innovative ideas and showcase their potential to the best of their abilities and compete with the best talent of the nation.
